VGR Driver Lineup

Our Drivers

Profiles and verified career highlights for the drivers representing VGR.

#99

Tyler Gonzalez

USA · St. Cloud, Florida2023 GR Cup Champion2025 MX-5 Cup Runner-upIMSA TCR Winner

Tyler Gonzalez is a multi-discipline sports-car racer and the inaugural 2023 Toyota GR Cup North America champion. He finished runner-up in the 2025 Whelen Mazda MX-5 Cup championship and earned IMSA Michelin Pilot Challenge TCR victories at Indianapolis and Road Atlanta in 2025 before winning at Sebring with VGR in 2026.

Tyler González es un piloto multidisciplinario y campeón inaugural de Toyota GR Cup North America en 2023. Fue subcampeón de Whelen Mazda MX-5 Cup en 2025 y logró victorias TCR de IMSA en Indianapolis y Road Atlanta en 2025, antes de ganar en Sebring con VGR en 2026.

Verified career source: IMSA
#21

William Tally

USA · GeorgiaOrthopedic SurgeonDaytona TCR WinnerRoad America Winner

William Tally combines professional racing with a career as an orthopedic surgeon. He entered IMSA competition in 2020, won the TCR class at Daytona in 2021 and added a Road America victory in 2024. His experience and consistency strengthen VGR’s No. 21 program.

William Tally combina el automovilismo profesional con su carrera como cirujano ortopédico. Entró a IMSA en 2020, ganó la clase TCR en Daytona en 2021 y sumó una victoria en Road America en 2024. Su experiencia y consistencia fortalecen el programa del auto #21.

Verified career source: IMSA
#99

Steven Clemons

USA · Greenville, South CarolinaSpec Miata BackgroundToyota GR Cup2025 GSX Race Winner

Steven Clemons began racing at age 17 after developing through the competitive Spec Miata ranks. He expanded into Toyota GR Cup and IMSA competition, then recorded two GSX victories in the 2025 VP Racing SportsCar Challenge before joining VGR’s CUPRA program for 2026.

Steven Clemons comenzó a competir a los 17 años tras desarrollarse en la competitiva categoría Spec Miata. Luego pasó a Toyota GR Cup e IMSA, y consiguió dos victorias GSX en VP Racing SportsCar Challenge durante 2025 antes de unirse al programa CUPRA de VGR en 2026.

Verified career source: IMSA
#21

Caleb Bacon

USAIMSA Series ReturneeLamborghini Super Trofeo ExperienceVGR #21

Caleb Bacon is an experienced sports-car racer with appearances in Lamborghini Super Trofeo and IMSA competition. He returned to the Michelin Pilot Challenge in 2026 to join VGR’s No. 21 CUPRA Leon VZ TCR program, contributing endurance experience and adaptable racecraft.

Caleb Bacon es un piloto con experiencia en Lamborghini Super Trofeo y competencias de IMSA. Regresó al Michelin Pilot Challenge en 2026 para integrarse al programa del CUPRA Leon VZ TCR #21 de VGR, aportando experiencia de resistencia y versatilidad.

Verified career source: IMSA
